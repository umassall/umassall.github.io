Direct Fractals

One of the pictures below can be expanded by clicking on it. A small region of a picture can be zoomed in on by clicking and dragging with the mouse. Hit the SPACEBAR to reset the region viewed on the current picture, and hit ENTER to return to the original collection of pictures. Zooming can also be done with the number keypad. 5 zooms in, 0 zooms out, and 1,2,3,4,6,7,8,9 pan in each of the eight cardinal directions (e.g. 1 shows the region south-west of the current one, 4 shows the west, etc).
