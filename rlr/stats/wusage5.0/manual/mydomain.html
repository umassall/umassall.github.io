mydomain

mydomain        com   (or org, or edu, or...)
mydomain indicates the top-level domain name of your site. For instance, if your site is in the boutell.com domain, your top-level domain is com. Since local machines are usually logged without a domain name, this option is needed to supply a domain name for such machines. Note: if you are using the subdomains option, you may specify one of those subdomains for the mydomain option.
