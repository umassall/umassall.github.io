topdomains

topdomains              10              (or another number, or all)
The topdomains option indicates how many of the highest-ranked domains should be shown in each report. By default, the top ten domains are shown. The sorting order is determined by the order option. You may specify any whole number of domains, or the word all. To turn off site reporting altogether, use the domains option. To combine related domains under one name, use the domaingroups option.
