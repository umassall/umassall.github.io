topproxydomains

 
topproxydomains           10              (or another number, or all)
The topproxydomains option indicates how many of the highest-ranked proxy domains should be shown in each report. By default, if the proxydomains option is turned on, the top ten proxy sites are shown. The sorting order is determined by the order option. You may specify any whole number of proxy domains, or the word all. To turn off proxy domain reporting altogether, use the proxydomains option.
