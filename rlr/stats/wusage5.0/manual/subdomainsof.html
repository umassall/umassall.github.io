subdomainsof

subdomainsof com
OR, for an Intranet server: 
subdomainsof mycompany.com
The subdomainsof option, which is new in Wusage 5.0, indicates that Wusage should treat every subdomain of the specified domain as a top-level domain in the domains report. This option is intended to replace the relatively clumsy subdomains option, which is kept for backwards compatibility. This option is especially useful for company Intranets, on which most accesses come from subdomains within the company and not from other top-level domains. If your server is not accessed exclusively from within the domain in question, you may also wish to use the allowsites option to exclude outside accesses from your analysis. If the subdomainsof option and the subdomains option are both absent, only top-level domains will appear in the domains listing and pie chart.
