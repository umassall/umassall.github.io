topsites

 
topsites                10              (or another number, or all)
The topsites option indicates how many of the highest-ranked sites should be shown in each report. By default, the top ten sites are shown. The sorting order is determined by the order option. You may specify any whole number of sites, or the word all. To turn off site reporting altogether, use the sites option.
