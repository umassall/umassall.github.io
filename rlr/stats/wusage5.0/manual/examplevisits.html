examplevisits

examplevisits             10              (or another number)
The examplevisits option indicates how many individual visits to the server should be included in the visits report. These visits are chosen to represent a cross-section of activity on the server, including visits of a variety of different lengths. By default, ten example visits are shown. You may specify any whole number of visits.
