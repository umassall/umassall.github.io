nocase
The nocase option, when it is present, instructs wusage to ignore differences in case when comparing URLs. Normally, URLs are case-sensitive. However, if your operating system does not make a distinction between uppercase and lowercase filenames, you may wish to use this option to combine URLs that actually retrieve the same document.
