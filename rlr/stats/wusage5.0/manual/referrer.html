Referrer Reporting Options

You must specify the referrers option to turn on this report. Your server logs must contain referrer (referring URL) information. See the referrers option for details.

ignorereferrers
referrers
referrersinvisits
topreferrers
The alternate spelling referers is also accepted for all referrer-related options.
