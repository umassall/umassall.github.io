topproxysites

 
topproxysites           10              (or another number, or all)
The topproxysites option indicates how many of the highest-ranked proxy sites should be shown in each report. By default, if the proxysites option is present at all, the top ten proxy sites are shown. The sorting order is determined by the order option. You may specify any whole number of proxy sites, or the word all. To turn off proxy site reporting altogether, use the proxysites option.
