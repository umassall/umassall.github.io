visits

 
visits on
visits off
If present, the visits option indicates that wusage should output information about individual visits to the server. Wusage estimates the duration of a visit by examining the amount of time between document requests from the same site. Although this method is not perfect, these estimates are normally quite accurate and useful.

When the visits option is turned on, wusage generates a list of "example" visits to the server. These visits are of a variety of durations and represent a cross-section of user activity on your server. You can control the number of example visits in the report using the examplevisits option.

Visits of at least a certain length are considered to be trails, and considered for inclusion in the trails report depending on their popularity.

The visits option defaults to on.

See also examplevisits, trailtimeout and trails.
