topreferrers

topreferrers             10              (or another number, or all)
The topreferrers option indicates how many of the most frequently reported "referrer" URLs should be shown in each report. By default, the top ten referrers are shown. The sorting order is determined by the order option. You may specify any whole number of referring URLs or the word all.
