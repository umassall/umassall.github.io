trailtimeout

trailtimeout             5              (whole number, 1 or more)
The trailtimeout option indicates how many minutes can elapse between document requests from a particular site before the trail is considered to have come to an end. This option affects the trails report and the visits report. By default, wusage assumes an individual visit has come to an end if more than 5 minutes elapse between document requests from that site. This can be adjusted by setting trailtimeout to a different number of minutes. For instance, if you know that most of your documents are very large and take a long time to download, you might wish to set trailtimeout to a larger value. You may specify any whole number of minutes. See also the trailminimumsteps option.
