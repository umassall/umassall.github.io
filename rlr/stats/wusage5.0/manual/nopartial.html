nopartial
The nopartial option, when it is present, instructs wusage not to generate a report for the current day, week or month in progress. By default, Wusage 5.0 does generate such a report and include it in the index, although it is not included in the historical graphs since the data is incomplete. If you typically have almost no data for the new day, week or month in your log when you run wusage, you may wish to use the nopartial option to turn off these "incomplete" reports.
