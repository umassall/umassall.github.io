trailminimumsteps

trailminimumsteps             3              (whole number, 1 or more)
The trailminimumsteps option indicates how many steps must be followed in a user's visit for that "trail" to be included in the top trails report. (All trails are considered to be visits, but only trails of at least the minimum number of steps are displayed on the popular-trails report.) By default, wusage sets this option to 3, because shorter trails are usually not of interest. However, you may wish to set this option to 2, or to a higher value. You may specify any whole number of steps. See also the trailtimeout option.
