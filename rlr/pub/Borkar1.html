Actor-Critic algorithm as multi-time scale stochastic approximation algorithm

Borkar, Vivek , Vijaymohan R. Konda
Actor-Critic algorithm as multi-time scale stochastic approximation algorithm
'Sadhana', Indian Academy of Sciences (Postscript - 550 KB )

Abstract: The actor-critic algorithm of Barto et al for simulation-based optimization of Markov decision processes is cast as a two time scale stochastic approximation. Convergence analysis, approximation issues and an example are studied. Keywords: Actor-critic algorithm, stochastic approximation, Markov decision processes, simulation-based algorithms, policy iteration.
