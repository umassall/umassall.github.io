Analysis and Design of Robot's Behavior: Towards a Methodology

Faihe, Yassine , Jean-Pierre Muller
Analysis and Design of Robot's Behavior: Towards a Methodology
Proceedings of the Sixth European Workshop on Learning Robots (EWLR6) ( gzipped Postscript - 116KB )

Abstract: We introduce a methodology to design reinforcement based control architectures for autonomous robots. It aims at systematizing the behavior analysis and the controller design. The methodology has to be seen as a conceptual framework in which a number of methods are to be defined. In this paper we use some more or less known methods to show the feasibility of the methodology. The postman-robot case study illustrates how the proposed methodology is applied.
